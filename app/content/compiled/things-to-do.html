Things To Do

Staying for a few days? Here are some ideas of activities and attractions:

  • Golf
  • Glentress for walks and mountain biking
  • Walks: Three Brethern, Eildon Hills
  • Abbotsford House, the home of author Sir Walter Scott
  • Scott's View, the author's favourite place
  • Melrose Abbey, where Robert the Bruce's heart is believed to be buried
  • Floors Castle, where Tarzan The Legend of Greystoke was filmed
  • Hadrian's Wall
  • St. Mary's Loch
  • Glenkinchie (?) Distillery
  • Edinburgh
  • Bannockburn
